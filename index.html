About Me

Jasmine Ng Labrador is a web-developer, comic-artist, and musician in Riverside, California. She brings frameworks to life with the power of HTML, CSS, and Javascript. When she's not developing websites, you can find her in a coffee shop working either working on ink and marker comics or writing songs. Her music taste is in constant rotation, but she unabashedly loves Silverstein and Hawthorne Heights.
